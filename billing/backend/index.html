How We Work

Our Proven Project Process

A transparent, structured approach from first conversation to post-launch support — so you always know exactly where your project stands.

1
Step 01

Consultation & Requirement Gathering

We begin with an in-depth discovery call to understand your business, goals, target audience, and technical needs — laying the foundation for everything that follows.

2
Step 02

Planning & Strategy

Our team crafts a detailed project plan — technology stack selection, timeline, milestones, resource allocation, and a clear roadmap aligned with your objectives.

3
Step 03

Wireframe / Prototype Creation

We design interactive wireframes and clickable prototypes giving you a visual walkthrough of the final product before any development begins.

4
Step 04

Client Approval & 50% Payment

Once you approve the prototype and project plan, we formalise the agreement and collect 50% of the project fee to initiate full-scale development.

5
Step 05

Development Phase

Our developers build your solution using industry best practices — clean code, modular architecture, version control, and regular internal reviews to ensure quality.

6
Step 06

Progress Updates (Every 25%)

You receive structured progress reports at every 25% milestone — screenshots, demo links, and changelogs — keeping you informed and in control throughout.

7
Step 07

Testing & Quality Assurance

Rigorous multi-device, cross-browser testing, performance benchmarking, and security audits ensure a polished, bug-free product before launch.

8
Step 08

Deployment

We handle server setup, domain configuration, SSL certificates, CI/CD pipelines, and a smooth zero-downtime go-live — your product launches with confidence.

9
Step 09

Post-launch Support & Revisions

Our relationship doesn't end at launch. We provide ongoing support, performance monitoring, content updates, and revisions to ensure your product continues to evolve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Transparent answers to the questions we hear most — so you can make informed decisions with confidence.

We build a wide spectrum of digital products: business websites, landing pages, e-commerce stores, custom web applications (CRM, ERP, booking systems, portals), cross-platform mobile apps (iOS & Android), WhatsApp automation systems, and admin dashboards. Whether you need something straightforward or highly complex, we have the expertise to deliver it.

Our stack is modern and versatile. For web frontend we use HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React.js, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS. Backend technologies include Node.js, Express, Python (Django/FastAPI), and PHP (Laravel). For mobile apps we use React Native and Flutter. Databases include MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Firebase. We deploy on AWS, GCP, Vercel, and cPanel hosting depending on project requirements.

Timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. A basic website or landing page typically takes 1–2 weeks. A custom web application or e-commerce store usually takes 4–8 weeks. Mobile apps generally require 8–16 weeks. We always provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase and keep you updated at every 25% milestone throughout development.

We follow a milestone-based payment structure. After the project plan is finalised and the prototype is approved, we collect 50% upfront to commence development. The remaining balance is due upon project completion and final delivery. For ongoing maintenance retainers, we offer flexible monthly or quarterly plans. All pricing is transparent — no hidden fees.
